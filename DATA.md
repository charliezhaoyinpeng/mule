```
data

|  |_ image_000002.jpg
|  |_ ...
|_ [AVA train_val video name 1]

|  |_ image_000002.jpg
|  |_ ...
|_ ...
|_ [AVA train_val video name 298]
   |_ image_000001.jpg
   |_ image_000002.jpg
   |_ ...
```
